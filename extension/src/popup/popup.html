Read markers are the green vertical bars to the left of paragraphs, which show which paragraphs SWDI Reader thinks you have read. Paragraphs get marked as read as you scroll past them. How long a paragraph has to stay in view before it counts is set by the reading speed control in Settings. This toggle only shows or hides the markers; paragraphs are tracked in the background either way.

Right-click a paragraph and choose "I've read this far" to mark everything above it read and everything below unread, so you resume exactly there next time.

Sync

Your reading stays on this device until you turn sync on, and the extension is complete without it. Connecting is never destructive: the reading on this device merges with whatever the key already holds, and nothing is lost on either side. Synced data is end to end encrypted; the sync key never leaves your devices.

This key opens your reading on other devices and the dashboard. Keep it private, ideally in a password manager; the dashboard's connect form can save it there for you.
