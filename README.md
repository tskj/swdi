# SWDI: The Sustainable Web & Internet Donations Initiative ("Sweedy")

**Ever wished you could easily and meaningfully give back to the creators whose
work enriches your online life? SWDI is an open-source initiative to make that
wish a reality, fostering a more wholesome, transparent, and user-controlled way
to support the content you love.**

Many of us _want_ to support good content, but current methods are often
inconvenient, intrusive, or don't feel right. SWDI bridges that gap.

## What is SWDI? The Elevator Pitch

Imagine your genuine appreciation for online articles, blogs, and open-source
projects effortlessly translating into real support. SWDI is a system where:

1.  **You** set a single, comfortable monthly budget – no per-article decisions
    or constant "micro-payments." This is about your overall support.
2.  A privacy-respecting **tracker** (e.g., a browser extension) locally and
    privately notes the content you genuinely engage with.
3.  At month's end, your pre-set budget is proposed for distribution to those
    creators, reflecting _your_ engagement and preferences.
4.  **You** have the final, thoughtful say before any payments occur.
5.  Creators are discovered via a **community-driven registry** of their
    _existing_ donation links (PayPal, crypto, etc.) – no complex signup needed
    for them to benefit from your goodwill.

**SWDI is not another corporate intermediary or a microtransaction nightmare.
It's an open, community-driven tool designed to be owned, understood, and
trusted by its users, facilitating a flow of support you already wish to give.**

## Our Ethos: The SWDI Difference

- ✨ **Wholesome & Fair:** Built to foster a healthier internet economy, moving
  beyond intrusive ads or piecemeal charges.
- 🔒 **User-Owned & Controlled:** Your data, your budget, your choices. This
  isn't about being nickel-and-dimed; it's about mindful, aggregated support from
  a budget you define.
- 🛡️ **Privacy First:** Engagement tracking is local. Optional sync is
  end-to-end encrypted with keys only you hold.
- 🌐 **Open & Transparent:** All code is open-source. The registry and its
  mechanisms aim for clarity.
- 🤝 **Community Driven:** Built by and for people who believe in a better, more
  supportive web.
- 💡 **Simple & Understandable:** Designed for trust and ease of use, making it
  easy to act on your desire to contribute.

## How it Works (High Level)

SWDI consists of a few key components:

- **Engagement Tracker:** A browser extension (or other integrations) that
  locally logs content you engage with.
- **Creator Registry:** A distributed or centrally-managed (but open) database
  mapping content to creator payment information, largely crowdsourced from their
  existing payment links.
- **Dashboard App:** Your personal interface to set your monthly support budget,
  review engagement insights, and authorize distributions. You can even use
  third-party dashboards built on our open standards.
- **Payment Executor:** Facilitates sending funds from you _directly_ to
  creators using their existing platforms.
- **(Optional) E2EE Sync Service:** For syncing your engagement data across your
  devices, end-to-end encrypted.
